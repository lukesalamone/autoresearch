Analysis:
The experiments so far show that factorized embeddings work well for small models. The pretrained initialization gives the best MRR@10 (0.4519) but exceeds the 10MB limit. Smaller factorized models are achieving ~0.41 MRR@10 with sizes around 2.5-3.7MB. The current best is embed=48, hidden=128, 3 layers (0.4121 MRR@10, 3.69MB). I want to explore the architecture space more systematically: try larger embed_dims with fewer layers to see if we can get better MRR@10 while staying under 10MB, try more layers to see if it helps, and try different pooling strategies.
